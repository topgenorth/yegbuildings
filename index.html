YEG Buildings

About

This is just a simple little Android application to show the various historical buildings in the city of Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. This app utilizes the City of Edmonton's Open Data feed for historical buildings as the underlying source for the buildings. Hopefully in the future other sources of information can be utilized.
